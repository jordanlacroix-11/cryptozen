Storage

First you should know what are the cryptos how they work, how you can buy things with it or make a transfer to another crypto wallet. Storage is one of the key knowledge to crypto lovers because that money is yours which is in your hands. Not in a bank account or some investing capitals or brokers. The big improvement in cryptos that it held its liquidity with the option to store and move it safely and quckly without any third party company.

Hot or cold

You can reach your cryptos wherever you have network or wherever not.. Assets can be stored in hot wallets and cold wallets. In hot wallets they are connected to the internet with benefits of easy and faster purchases, but with cold wallets you have a more secure option tho save your cryptos with no internet connection.
